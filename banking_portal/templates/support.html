Frequently Asked Questions
How long does an international transfer take? ▼
Instant transfers arrive within minutes. Same-day (RTGS) transfers are processed within the same business day. Standard transfers take 1–3 business days depending on the destination country and local banking hours.
What are the transfer limits for my account? ▼
Standard accounts can transfer up to $10,000 per day and $50,000 per month. Premium accounts have limits of $50,000 per day and $200,000 per month. Limits can be temporarily increased by contacting our support team with verification.
Why was my transfer rejected or failed? ▼
Transfers can fail due to an invalid SWIFT/BIC code, incorrect IBAN, insufficient funds, or exceeding your daily limit. All failed transfers are automatically refunded within 24 hours. Check the transaction details for the specific rejection reason.
How do I complete KYC verification? ▼
Go to Profile → Personal Info and click "Verify Identity". You will need a valid government-issued ID (passport or driver's license) and a selfie. Verification is usually completed within 10 minutes during business hours.
How do I reset my password? ▼
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email. You will receive a secure reset link valid for 15 minutes. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
How do I block or freeze my card? ▼
Go to Profile → Security and toggle "Card Status" to freeze your card instantly. This blocks all new transactions while keeping your account active. You can unfreeze at any time. For permanent card cancellation, contact our 24/7 support line.
I noticed an unauthorized transaction. What should I do? ▼
Immediately freeze your card via Profile → Security. Then contact our 24/7 fraud team at +1 (800) 926-2265. We investigate all fraud claims within 24 hours and provide provisional credit within 5 business days if the transaction is confirmed fraudulent.
What are Z Bank's fees? ▼
Z Bank has zero monthly account fees for standard accounts. International instant transfers cost $5, same-day transfers cost $2, and standard transfers are free. There are no ATM withdrawal fees at partner ATMs worldwide. Full fee schedule is available in Settings.
