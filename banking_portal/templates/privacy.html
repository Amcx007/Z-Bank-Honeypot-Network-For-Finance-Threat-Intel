Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026 · Effective immediately

At Z Bank, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our banking services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, make a transaction, or contact support.

  • Personal identification — full name, date of birth, government ID
  • Contact details — email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Financial information — account numbers, transaction history, balances
  • Device and usage data — IP address, browser type, login timestamps

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to provide and improve our banking services. We do not sell your data to third parties.

  • Process transactions and maintain your account
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Send account notifications and security alerts
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Improve our products and customer experience

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including 256-bit SSL encryption, multi-factor authentication, and continuous fraud monitoring.

All data is stored in encrypted form and access is strictly limited to authorised personnel only.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as required by applicable law. Transaction records are kept for a minimum of 7 years in compliance with financial regulations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also request a copy of all data we hold about you.

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and secure. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.

Questions about your privacy?

Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@zbank.com or write to us at Z Bank Financial Services, 142 Finance Street, New York, NY 10001.
